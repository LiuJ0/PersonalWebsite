FocusMath

FocusMath was an idea first conceived during the COVID-19 Pandemic. During this time, we found that learning math became much more difficult. In person, a teacher can advise you by correcting your mistakes, but online, it's much more difficult. FocusMath allows students to take pictures of math equations that they've worked through to solve. Then, it checks the student's work to help reinforce their learning.

Return STEM

Return STEM is a 501c3 nonprofit dedicated to bringing programming education to students worldwide. Our unique approach to teaching programming stems from the frustration that many students endure: the transition between introductory programming concepts and intermediate programming projects. At Return STEM, we hope to provide education that enables students to discover the joy of programming by creating their own projects.
